Reforming Turtle Races for Conservation

The Problem

Over 90% of turtle races failed to meet basic animal care and conservation standards in 2021

The Solution: You

As a turtle race organizer, you are powerfully positioned to help the practice of turtle racing adopt a Conservation Ethic

Conservation Partnerships

The Turtle Race Task Force has the expertise needed to help you revolutionize your race while keeping it fun

Key Concepts

Development of a Conservation Ethic for turtle races revolves around the following Six Key Concepts. The Turtle Race Task Force is currently developing Best Practices guidelines based on each concept.

Officials guidelines will be released by February 2022.
